About Us

Founded in 2010, Frullato started with a simple mission: to create delicious, nutritious smoothies using only the freshest ingredients. What began as a small cart at the local farmers' market has grown into a beloved smoothie destination.

We believe that what you put into your body matters. That's why we source only high-quality fruits and superfoods, working with local farmers whenever possible.
